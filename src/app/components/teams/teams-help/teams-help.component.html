This page allows you to manage teams in the current program. Click the Generate New Teams button to automatically create a specified number of new teams. You can also manually add a new team by clicking the Add New Team button.

After creating teams, you can view and edit the team details by clicking on the team name. On the team edit screen, you can change the team name, select team coaches, and add outside coaches who are not parents/guardians of team players.

Within each team roster, you can click on a player name to view and edit player details. In the Options column, you can click the remove button to Remove the player from the team, or click the transfer button to move the player to a different team.

After making at least one trade, a new button will appear which will allow you to view the trade history for the current program. From this view, you can delete individual trades or clear all trade history for the program.
