This page allows you to perform certain maintenance tasks for the selected program.

Once evaluations have been entered, you can process them to calculate player evaluation scores. Each time you process evaluations, the score and looks for each player will be updated and replaced.

Once evaluations have been processed, you can auto-assign players to teams. This will assign players to teams based on their evaluation scores. Players are assigned in descending order of their evaluation score, from the first team to the last team. In the next pass, the team order is reversed. This continues until all players have been assigned to a team. Each time you auto-assign players to teams, the team rosters will be cleared first.

You can also remove all players from teams. This will remove all players from all teams, but will not delete the teams themselves. This will also remove all coach assignments.

Finally, you can reset the program. This will remove all players, teams, evaluations, and coach assignments. This will not delete the program itself. Please note that this operation cannot be undone.
